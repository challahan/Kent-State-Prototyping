Financing Your Education at Kent State

In This Photo

The Kent State cheerleaders performing on the Risman Plaza.



Paying for college is an important aspect of realizing your academic goals. Kent State's affordable tuition helps you keep costs low as you pursue your education. In addition, Kent State University is ranked as one of top public research universities in the United States. Kent State will provide a high-quality education at an affordable price.

This information reflects at-a-glance, generalized ballpark rates and excludes certain fees and weighted numbers based on your admit type, such as a new freshman or graduate student.

Undergraduate

  • Ohio resident: $19,352
  • Non-Ohio resident: $27,312


Includes estimated tuition and room and board for a full-time undergraduate student at the Kent Campus based on 11-16 semester hours, a standard double room and a basic meal plan. Program and special course fees are additional. A $447 per credit-hour fee applies to all enrolled semester hours over 16.

The fees and estimates are intended to act as a guide. Actual fees may vary based on chosen plans. Other expenses not included in this total include an enrollment fee of $150 for Kent Campus freshmen and the following estimates: books $1,400; transportation $1,744; miscellaneous expenses $2,430.

Non-Ohio residents, ask about our University Award.

Graduate

  • Ohio resident: $13,811
  • Non-Ohio resident: $16,889


Includes estimated tuition for a full-time graduate student at the Kent Campus based on 9 semester hours. Program and special course fees are additional.

The fees and estimates are intended to act as a guide. Actual fees may vary based on chosen plans.

International

  • Undergraduate: $30,176
  • Graduate: $27,106

Includes estimated tuition for a full-time student (one academic year, two semesters), living expenses, medical insurance and other books and supplies.

The fees and estimates are intended to act as a guide. Actual fees may vary based on chosen plans.

For more information about expenses, visit the International Admissions website.
