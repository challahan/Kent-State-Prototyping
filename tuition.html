Besides being one of the best universities in Ohio, Kent State is also financially accessible to most students. Check out the Kent State tuition rates below. Kent State is happy to work with students to make college as affordable as possible.


2013 - 2014 Fees

Includes estimated tuition and room and board for a full-time undergraduate student at the Kent Campus based on 11 -16 semester hours. Program and special course fees are additional. A $447 per credit-hour fee applies to all enrolled semester hours over 16.

Ohio resident: $19,352
Non-Ohio resident: $27,312

The fees and estimates on this page are intended to act as a guide. Actual fees may vary based on chosen plans. Other expenses not included in this total include an enrollment fee of $150 for Kent Campus freshmen and the following estimates: books $1,400; transportation $1,744; miscellaneous expenses $2,430. Go to the Bursar’s Office website for more detailed information.
