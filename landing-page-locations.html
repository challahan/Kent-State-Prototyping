The Kent State eight-campus system is situated in northeastern Ohio amid rolling terrain dotted by spring-fed lakes, a short drive from four major urban centers, with airports offering access to the nation and the world. In addition, education abroad and international exchange programs offer you the opportunity to travel the world while getting the Kent State experience.

Campuses

Kent State University’s eight-campus system is one of the country’s largest. The Kent Campus offers all the resources of a large university in a quintessential Midwestern collegiate setting. Our seven Regional Campuses offer students the advantage of an internationally renowned university along with specialized programs designed to meet regional needs.

Education Abroad

Students wishing to include an international experience in their program can travel to Kent State campuses in Florence or Geneva. They can also work toward their degrees in Kent State classrooms in more than 20 other locations around the world.
