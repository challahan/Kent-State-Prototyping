Fitting In

With more than 22,000 undergraduate students and 5,500 graduate students enrolled from all 50 states and 100 countries, odds have it that all kinds of relationships and connections will be established -- peer to peer, classmate to classmate, classmate to professor.

Paying For It

More than 80 percent of Kent State students receive financial assistance via scholarships, grants, loans or work study.


Living on Campus

Make yourself at home at one of the 24 residence halls and an option of 12 residential learning communities. You’ll never go hungry with 22 on-campus restaurants, dining halls and coffee carts.


Joining In

Getting involved at Kent State is easy with a choice of more than 300 clubs and organizations, 1,000 programs, activities, and on-campus events, 18 sports teams, and 20 sororities and fraternities.


Alumni Pride

Kent State University has more than 214,000 alumni worldwide, representing all 50 states in the United States and more than 100 countries.
