100 Years. 280 Academic Programs.

Kent State University has been helping students fulfill their life goals for more than 100 years. As a top university in Ohio, Kent State has eight colleges offering more than 280 academic programs at the certificate, associate, bachelor's, master's, educational specialist and doctoral levels.
