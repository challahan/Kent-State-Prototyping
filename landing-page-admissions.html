Undergraduate Admissions

At Kent State, a top Ohio college, you can choose from among more than 300 majors and programs, undergraduate and graduate degrees, 24 sororities and fraternities, 18 NCAA Division I teams and thousands of programs, activities and events. Some 18,000 undergrads — from 100 countries and all 50 states — are realizing their dreams on the Kent Campus every day, and we have no doubt you’ll find where you belong too.

Graduate Admissions

Kent State University is a public research university that serves more than 6,000 graduate students enrolled in more than 150 graduate degree and certificate programs. Partnerships between students and our nationally and internationally renowned faculty extend scholarship in important areas of research, creativity and practice.

International Admissions

Join the approximately 2,000 international students from nearly 100 countries who are currently enrolled at Kent State. Whether you are an undergraduate or a graduate student, there is a place for you at Kent State.

Post-Secondary Enrollment Option Programs

The Post-secondary Enrollment Options Program (PSEOP) at Kent State University is designed to provide qualified public high school students with the opportunity to enhance their high school education with a true college experience.

Kent State’s online degrees and certificate programs are a great way to finish a degree or start a new one. Earning your degree online offers you a convenient, flexible way to take coursework.
