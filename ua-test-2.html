We Can’t Tell You Who to Become, But We Can Give You the Knowledge to Make Anything Possible. Dream Big.



Finding the right fit and a sense of community can take many forms. At Kent State, a top Ohio college, you can choose from among more than 280 majors and programs, undergraduate and graduate degrees, 24 sororities and fraternities, 18 NCAA Division I teams and 1,000 programs, activities and events. Some 18,000 undergrads — from 100 countries and all 50 states — are realizing their dreams on the Kent Campus every day, and we have no doubt you’ll find where you belong, too.

New Freshmen

A new freshman is someone who has not attended any college, university, technical or proprietary school after graduation from high school. Students who have taken college coursework while in high school should also apply here as a freshman.

New Freshmen

Transfer & Transitioning Students

A transfer student is someone who was enrolled at another college, university, technical or proprietary school after high school. A transitioning student is someone who is coming from a Kent State regional campus.

Transfer & Transitioning Students

Post-undergraduate Students

A post-undergraduate is a student who has already earned a bachelor's degree and who wants to take additional undergraduate coursework at Kent State.

Post-undergraduate Students

Guest Students

A guest student takes a limited number of undergraduate courses for credit, but is not pursuing a degree.
