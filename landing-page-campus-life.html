Activities & Organizations

Get involved on campus and customize your own version of the Kent State experience. The activities, organizations and events at Kent State University are as varied and intriguing as our students. Whether you live on campus or commute, at Kent State, your college experience will be unlike any other.

Arts & Culture

There’s always something new to experience at the many art events held at Kent State and off-campus locations year round. The College of the Arts provides many student-run productions, exhibitions and events.

Housing & Dining

About 6,500 students from a variety of backgrounds and locations live in campus housing. There's always someone new and interesting to meet right down the hall! Plus, when you live on campus, your dining choices are limited only by your imagination!

Explore More About Housing & Dining

Transportation

Whether you’re a current student, parent, visitor or considering attending Kent State University, you need to be able to get around campus. Parking Services is responsible for providing well-maintained parking lots as well as on-campus assistance to stranded motorists, while PARTA busses run a regular schedule to every part of campus.

Discover More About Transportation

Campus Safety

The safety of Kent State's students is paramount, which is why Kent State is kept safe by an award-winning police department. In addition, Residence Services security aides and campus escorts are only a phone call away!
