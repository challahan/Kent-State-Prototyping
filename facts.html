Kent State Students By the Numbers

In This Photo

Students gather in front of the Memorial Athletic & Convocation Center.

With more than 22,000 undergraduate students and 5,500 graduate students enrolled from all 50 states and 100 countries, odds have it that all kinds of relationships and connections will be established -- peer to peer, classmate to classmate, classmate to professor.

The Kent State eight-campus system is situated in northeastern Ohio amid rolling terrain dotted by spring-fed lakes, a short drive from four major urban centers, with airports offering access to the nation and the world. In addition, education abroad and international exchange programs offer you the opportunity to travel the world while getting the Kent State experience.
