Beyond Kent

Area Attractions

Northeast Ohio is home to a concentration of world-class attractions and activities that few other areas in the country can offer. World-class museums, modern sporting venues, an extensive theater complex, parks, fairs, festivals, shopping, and an international selection of dining options offer compelling answers to that most persistent of questions, “What do you want to do today?”

Museums

From the world-class museums of Cleveland’s University Circle area to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and Museum to the hard-hitting Pro Football Hall of Fame, the region’s museum collections explore a wide range of fascinating subjects. Explore your interests -there’s something for everyone!

  • The Cleveland Museum of Art
  • The Museum of Contemporary Art
  • The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and Museum
  • The Great Lakes Science Museum
  • The Western Reserve Historical Society and Crawford Auto/Aviation Museum
  • The Akron Art Museum
  • The Pro Football Hall of Fame

Theater

Did you know that Cleveland is home to the country’s second largest theater district? When also consider all of the region’s many public and community theaters, black box stages and other performance venues, it seems as if “all the world’s a stage” in Northeast Ohio.

  • Cleveland Theater Listings
  • Akron Theater Listings

Outdoor Activities

Get out! Enjoy spectacular scenery, hundreds of miles of trails for hiking and biking, or boating, canoeing, sailing, swimming and fishing in rivers, streams and lakes - including one of the Great Lakes. With miles of beaches, two extensive Metroparks systems, a national recreation area, two world-class zoos and the roller coaster capital of the world, Northeast Ohio invites visitors to explore the outdoors, get active and have fun.

  • Cleveland Metroparks
  • Cleveland Metroparks Zoo
  • Cuyahoga Valley National Recreation Area
  • Akron Metro Parks
  • Wildwater Kingdom
  • Cedar Point Amusement Park

Food & Drink

If you’re visiting Northeast Ohio, it’s good to come hungry. The region’s rich ethnic heritage has made it a dining destination for a wide range of specialty cuisine from all over the world, innovative modern dishes and fine, gourmet dining.

  • Dining in Cleveland
  • Dining in Akron

Shopping

From modern shopping centers, to classic mall shopping to a wide variety of unique shops, Northeast Ohio has what it takes for you to “get your shop on.”

  • Aurora Farms Premium Outlets
  • Hudson’s First and Main
  • Cleveland Shopping
  • Akron Shopping

Live Music/Comedy

Everyone knows that “Cleveland Rocks,” but Akron also rocks and so does Kent. In fact, the city of Kent is home to more than 22 performance venues. The region also knows what’s funny. Noted comedians, including Drew Carey, Arsenio Hall and John Caparulo are former Kent State students. You can catch the next rising stars, along with famous headliners at several area comedy clubs.

  • Cleveland Live Music
  • Akron Live Music
  • Hilarities at Pickwick and Frolic
  • The Cleveland Improv
  • The Funny Stop

Festivals & Unique Attractions

Ohio boasts one of the nation’s most varied and busy schedule of festivals. From agricultural fairs to art festivals or events celebrating ethnic heritage or Ohio food delicacies, there’s something interesting to experience nearly every weekend. There are also one-of-a-kind attractions and destinations that reveal history of the area and its residents.

  • Northern Ohio Festivals and Events
  • Akron Festivals and Events
  • Cleveland Botanical Gardens
  • “A Christmas Story” House
  • U.S.S. Cod WW II Submarine
  • Stan Hywet Hall and Gardens

Sports

Play ball! (Or drop the puck, nail a three pointer or drive to the end zone.) Join the superfans of Northeast Ohio as they cheer on their favorite professional, semi-professional and college teams at some of the country’s most modern sporting venues.

  • Cleveland Indians
  • Akron Rubber Ducks
  • Lake County Captains
  • Lake Erie Crushers
  • Cleveland Cavaliers
  • Cleveland Browns
  • Lake Erie Monsters
